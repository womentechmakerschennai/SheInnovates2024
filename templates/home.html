Difference between Acne, Eczema and Psoriasis

People always tend to get confused between the symptoms of Acne, Eczema and Psoriasis as they are often interconnected with each other. It's important to know the difference between them in order to understand these diseases.

Psoriasis

  • Patches of thick, red skin
  • Dry, cracked skin
  • Thick, ridged, pitted nails
  • Itchy skin

ACNE

  • Whiteheads
  • Blackheads
  • Papules
  • Pimples

Eczema

  • Yellow or white scaly patches
  • Itchy, greasy, or oily skin
  • Red rash
  • Hair loss
